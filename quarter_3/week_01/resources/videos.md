## How to Use These Videos

### For Students

**Before Class (Preview):**
- Watch the relevant video before the lesson
- Take notes on key concepts
- Write down questions to ask in class
- Try practice problems from video

**After Class (Review):**
- Rewatch sections you found challenging
- Pause and work through examples yourself
- Use videos to check your understanding
- Watch at 1.5x speed for review

**During Homework:**
- Keep video open for reference
- Rewind to specific sections as needed
- Try problem first, then check with video
- Use timestamps to find specific topics

**Test Preparation:**
- Review all Week 1 videos
- Focus on topics you find most difficult
- Work through practice problems alongside video
- Make study notes from video examples

### For Teachers

**Flipped Classroom:**
- Assign videos as homework night before
- Use class time for practice and questions
- Check understanding with quick formative assessment

**Differentiation:**
- Below Level (ACT 15-18): Assign videos before AND after lesson
- At Level (ACT 19-22): Optional review resource
- Above Level (ACT 23+): Use for independent study

**Homework Support:**
- Include video links in homework assignments
- Reference specific timestamps for challenging problems
- Encourage students to use videos at home
