## Differentiation with Videos

### For Below Level Students (ACT 15-18)
**Before Class:**
- Watch assigned video completely
- Take notes on key formulas
- Write 2-3 questions to ask in class

**After Class:**
- Rewatch challenging sections
- Pause video and work through examples
- Use timestamps to find specific help

### For At Level Students (ACT 19-22)
**Optional Use:**
- Watch if homework is confusing
- Use for test review
- Watch at 1.5x speed for quick review

### For Above Level Students (ACT 23+)
**Challenge:**
- Watch to reinforce concepts quickly
- Focus on why formulas work
- Explore extension topics mentioned in videos
