## COURSE OVERVIEW

**Course:** AP Precalculus
**Grade Level:** 12th
**Student Profile:** ACT Math scores 15-22 (below average to average)
**Schedule:** Block schedule - Mondays, Wednesdays, Alternating Fridays (90 minutes each)
**Total Sessions:** 34 class periods (approximately 51 hours of instruction)
**Semester Dates:** January 7, 2026 - May 1, 2026

---

## SEMESTER GOALS

### Primary Objectives:
1. Master unit circle trigonometry including all six functions
2. Graph and transform trigonometric functions
3. Develop proficiency with trigonometric identities
4. Solve trigonometric equations
5. Prepare students for AP Precalculus exam and improve ACT scores

### Student-Centered Goals:
- Build mathematical confidence through scaffolded instruction
- Develop problem-solving strategies applicable to ACT
- Create strong foundational understanding for college mathematics
- Improve from ACT 15-22 range toward 22-28 range

## DIFFERENTIATION STRATEGIES

### For Students Below Level (ACT 15-18):
1. **Extended Time:** Allow additional time on assessments
2. **Visual Supports:** Provide unit circle templates, formula cards
3. **Scaffolded Notes:** Partially completed notes with key concepts
4. **Small Group Instruction:** Targeted intervention during Friday sessions
5. **Technology Support:** Calculator use, online practice tools
6. **Peer Tutoring:** Partner with stronger students for practice

### For Students At Level (ACT 19-22):
1. **Standard Pacing:** Follow curriculum as designed
2. **Regular Practice:** Complete all assigned problems
3. **Study Groups:** Encourage collaborative learning
4. **ACT Practice:** Focus on problem-solving strategies
5. **Application Problems:** Emphasize real-world connections

### For Students Above Level (ACT 23+):
1. **Challenge Problems:** Extend with calculus preview problems
2. **Independent Projects:** Allow deeper exploration of topics
3. **Peer Teaching:** Lead small group discussions
4. **Competition Math:** Introduce AMC/Math League problems
5. **Early Introduction:** Preview upcoming topics
