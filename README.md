## ğŸ¯ Course Topics by Quarter

### Quarter 3 (Weeks 1-10)
1. **Week 1**: Right Triangle Trigonometry, Radians, Unit Circle Introduction
2. **Week 2**: Unit Circle - All Quadrants, Special Angles
3. **Week 3**: Graphing Sine and Cosine
4. **Week 4**: Transformations of Trig Functions
5. **Week 5**: Graphing Tangent, Cotangent, Secant, Cosecant
6. **Week 6**: Inverse Trig Functions
7. **Week 7**: Trig Identities and Equations
8. **Week 8**: Sum/Difference and Double-Angle Formulas
9. **Week 9**: Law of Sines and Law of Cosines
10. **Week 10**: Applications and Review

### Quarter 4 (Weeks 1-6)
1. **Week 1**: Vectors and Parametric Equations
2. **Week 2**: Polar Coordinates and Graphs
3. **Week 3**: Complex Numbers and De Moivre's Theorem
4. **Week 4**: Sequences and Series
5. **Week 5**: AP Exam Review
6. **Week 6**: AP Exam Preparation and Practice Tests
