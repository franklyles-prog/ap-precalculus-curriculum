## CURRICULUM PACING GUIDE OVERVIEW

With 34 class sessions (51 hours), the suggested pacing is:

1. **Unit 1: Foundations & Unit Circle** (Sessions 1-11, Jan 7 - Feb 6)
   - Right triangle trig review
   - Radians and arc length
   - Unit circle construction and memorization
   - All six trig functions

2. **Unit 2: Graphing Trigonometric Functions** (Sessions 12-20, Feb 9 - Mar 11)
   - Sine and cosine graphs
   - Transformations (amplitude, period, phase shift, vertical shift)
   - Tangent, cotangent, secant, cosecant graphs
   - Midterm review and exam

3. **Unit 3: Trigonometric Identities** (Sessions 21-29, Mar 13 - Apr 15)
   - Pythagorean identities
   - Sum and difference formulas
   - Double and half-angle formulas
   - Verifying identities

4. **Unit 4: Inverse Functions & Equations** (Sessions 30-34, Apr 17 - Apr 29)
   - Inverse trig functions
   - Solving trig equations
   - Applications
   - Semester review
